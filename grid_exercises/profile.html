My favorite book is One Fish, Two Fish, Red Fish, Blue Fish by Doctor Suess! It's a really good read and I would highly recommend it. It was published March 12, 1960, and has since become one of the best selling children's books of all time.

This book has a lot of rhyming and a fun rhythm. It mentions two characters, Jay and Kay, and all their amazing animal friends.
